Integration

Live in 15 minutes, not 15 days

One WebSocket endpoint. Raw audio in, AI audio out. That's it.

1

Connect via WebSocket

Open a WebSocket to our endpoint. Send a JSON start event with the call_id and lead data. We handle everything else — STT, LLM, TTS — in one pipeline.

2

Stream Audio Both Ways

Send raw mulaw 8kHz audio frames from the phone call. We stream back AI speech in real-time. No JSON wrapping on audio — just raw bytes, minimum latency.

3

Get Results via Webhook

After each call, we POST full results to your webhook — transcript, disposition, duration, transfer status. Plug it straight into your CRM or dialer.
